Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

CPMS (Clinic Patient Management System) is designed for small clinics and medical practitioners. We are committed to protecting the privacy of your patients and your clinic data. This policy explains how data is handled within this application.

2. Data Storage

All patient data — including names, phone numbers, medical history, visit records, prescriptions, and attached images — is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. No patient data is transmitted to any external server or third party by default.

3. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive Backup, your data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption before being uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. Only you can access this backup. We do not have access to your Google account or the backup files.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera & Storage: Used to capture and store patient photos and medical images.
  • Internet: Used only when Google Drive Backup is enabled.
  • Google Account: Used only for authenticating Google Drive Backup. We do not access any other Google services or data.

5. Patient Data Responsibility

As a medical practitioner using this application, you are responsible for ensuring that patient data is handled in compliance with applicable local health data regulations (such as the Digital Personal Data Protection Act in India). You must obtain appropriate consent from patients before recording their medical information.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete any patient's data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the application will remove all local data from your device. If Google Drive Backup is enabled, you can delete backups from the Backup & Restore screen.

7. Children's Privacy

This application may contain medical records of minor patients as part of clinical practice. This data is protected under the same local storage policy and is never shared externally without your explicit action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the app version release notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the Contact section of this site, or email contact.mahendrar@gmail.com.
