Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is my patient data safe?

Yes. All data is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to any server unless you enable Google Drive Backup, which encrypts data before uploading.

What happens if I lose my phone?

If you have Google Drive Backup enabled, you can restore all your data on a new device by signing in with the same Google account and restoring from backup.

Can multiple doctors use the same app?

Currently CPMS supports single-user mode. Multi-user support is planned for a future version.

Can I print prescriptions?

Yes. Prescriptions can be exported as PDFs and shared via WhatsApp, email, or any sharing app. You can also print directly from the share menu.

I forgot my PIN. What do I do?

For security reasons, PINs cannot be recovered. You will need to uninstall and reinstall the app. If you have a backup, you can restore your data afterward.
